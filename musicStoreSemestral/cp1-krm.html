Relational model for musicstore database

  • User (nickname, email)
  • PrivateInfo (fullName, address, user, phoneNumber, password)
    • FK: (user) ⊆ User(nickname)
    • address (privateInfo, street, city, zip)
      • FK: (privateInfo) ⊆ PrivateInfo(phoneNumber)
  • WorkerUser (personalIdentificationNumber, nickname, email)
    • FK: (nickname, email) ⊆ User(nickname, email)
  • CustomerUser (nickname, email, cookies, premiumStatus)
    • FK: (nickname, email) ⊆ User(nickname, email)
  • Store (location)
  • Product (ISBN, modelNumber, manufacturer)
  • InstrumentProduct (ISBN, modelNumber, range, type, pickupType)
    • pickupType(instrumentProduct)
      • FK: (instrumentProduct) ⊆ InstrumentProduct(ISBN)
    • FK: (ISBN, modelNumber) ⊆ Product(ISBN, modelNumber)
  • PASystemProduct (ISBN, modelNumber, range, type, power)
    • FK: (ISBN, modelNumber) ⊆ Product(ISBN, modelNumber)
  • Acessory (ISBN, type)
